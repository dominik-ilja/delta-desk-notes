# My Responsibilities

These are my main responsibilities:

- Clients
    - Reviewing tickets that are submitted through the channels
    - Prompt communication through email, teams, slack, etc.
    - Sending updates of our projects
    - Gathering requirements for our products:
        - What bugs/issues are happening?
        - What features/improvements need to be made?
- Developers
    - Assigning tasks
    - Communicating with developers
        - Telling them what needs done
        - Answering questions
        - Getting updates on what they done
- Project Management
    - Prioritizing the work to be done
    - Creating overarching projects
    - Understanding projects
    - Delegating tasks to other people that would be a waste of time for me to do
    - Documenting our projects

---

I need to gather up all the different information I receive, synthesize it, and then send it to the correct places. 

1. I take the various data and requirements that come from:
    - Emails
    - Meetings
    - Messages
2. They get processed through discussions and scrutiny
3. Finally, they get turned into ClickUp tickets or tasks
